GridDown

Apocalypse-proof offline maps

Maps of the United States — streets, forest service roads, trails, and terrain — that keep working with no internet at all. Built as an off-grid, emergency replacement for online maps.

  • Download states while you're online, from the Map library.
  • Everything then runs 100% offline — no signal needed.
  • Toggle Day/Night and Terrain (hillshade + contours).

Map data © OpenStreetMap contributors. Elevation: USGS / Terrain Tiles.
